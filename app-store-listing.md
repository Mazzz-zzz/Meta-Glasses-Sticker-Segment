# AutoStickers – App Store Listing Draft

## Title
AutoStickers – Glasses to Stickers

## Subtitle
Turn Ray-Ban Meta captures into instant, shareable stickers.

## Description
AutoStickers transforms photos and clips from your Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses into clean, cutout stickers—automatically. Capture, peel, and share in seconds with AI-powered subject detection and a single tap export.

- One-tap peel: AI cutouts from glasses captures, no manual masking
- Holographic sticker styles: glossy, matte, neon, and transparent edges
- Batch export: share packs to Messages, Instagram, and WhatsApp
- Smart cleanup: edge smoothing, background suppression, and color pop
- Library sync: import from Photos and Files, not just glasses
- Privacy-first: processing tied to your account; you control what’s saved

## What’s New (launch)
- Faster peel speed on Ray-Ban captures
- New holo edge style
- Sharper edge smoothing for portraits
